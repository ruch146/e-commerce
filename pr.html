ABOUT US

Welcome to Shoppers Stop, your number one source for Clothing. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of our product, with a focus on three characteristics ie: dependability, customer service and uniqueness. Founded in 2001 by Aniket Salunke,Ruchika Kukreja & Priyanka Lade Shoppers stop has come a long way . When their team first started out, their passion for helping others to be more eco-friendly by providing the best clothing drove them to do intense research and gave them the impetus to turn hard work and inspiration into to a booming online store. We now serve customers all over India, and are thrilled to be a part of the eco-friendly wing of the fashion industry.
We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
